Give the gift of ether!

Ether.Cards are cryptocurrency gift cards that make it incredibly easy to gift tokens to one another. Want to distribute your token or introduce someone to cryptocurrencies? Ether.Cards is the best way to get that done without the recipient needing much technical know-how.

Buy

Gift tokens in style by choosing your favourite design from our store or create your own custom cards here.

Load

Load an Ether Card with tokens using any wallet that supports scanning QR codes.

Redeem

Redeem your tokens by using MyCrypto or any mobile wallet that has a seed word restoration feature.
